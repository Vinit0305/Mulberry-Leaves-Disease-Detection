Suspected Disease: Flacheria

Causes:

  1. Excessive temperature
  2. Poor ventilation
  3. Poor hygiene
  4. Improper bedding sanitation
  5. Accumulation of waste
  6. Feeding on contaminated leaves
  7. Overcrowding of insects

Symptoms:

  1. Slow movement
  2. Lack of appetite
  3. Flattening of the abdominal region
  4. Swollen chest area
  5. Liquid vomiting
  6. Loss of grip strength
  7. The body becomes soft and ruptures
  8. Blackening of the chest

Control Measures:

  1. Effective disinfection of rearing houses/equipment
  2. Avoid overcrowding
  3. Maintain optimal temperature and relative humidity
  4. Avoid feeding diseased and contaminated leaves
  5. Collect and destroy diseased insects
  6. Apply bleaching powder + lime in a 1:9 ratio
  7. Proper bedding sanitation
  8. Proper hygiene
