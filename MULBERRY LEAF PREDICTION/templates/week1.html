Week 1: Mulberry Lifecycle

Early Stage of Mulberry Plant

In the first week, mulberry saplings are introduced and nurtured. Proper planting and care at this stage ensure strong root development and robust growth.

Key Development Features

Aspect Description
Soil Preparation Well-drained loamy soil enriched with compost or manure.
Watering Regular but controlled watering to avoid waterlogging.
Sunlight Proper spacing for optimal sunlight exposure.
Pest Management Use of organic methods to protect young saplings.

Care Instructions Summary

  • Use well-prepared, nutrient-rich soil.
  • Maintain consistent watering without over-saturation.
  • Ensure full exposure to sunlight for at least 6 hours daily.
  • Inspect for pests and apply eco-friendly repellents if needed.
